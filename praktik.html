Praktik

[ prak-teek ]

Internship

It’s the direct translation of the word internship. But it doesn’t look the same in Sweden as anywhere else. Because of the flat organizations and lack of hierarchy an internship in Sweden is often joyous. You’ll get to learn what you set out to and no one will treat you like an intern. But there is a but, and I cannot lie – it’s big. No one gets paid, not even travel expenses are covered.

Use this word in a sentence

På praktik får man praktisera det man lärt sig i skolan.

During an internship you get to try out what you've learned in school.
