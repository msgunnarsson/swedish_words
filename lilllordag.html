Lillördag

[ lill loerdaag ]

Little Saturday

Lillördag is a nickname for Wednesday and means Little Saturday. It's a day for party and drinking but in a lighter way than Saturday. The word stems from the fact that Wednesday was the day all maids used to have time off, since they had to work on Saturdays.

Use this word in a sentence

Vill du ha ett glas vin? Det är ju ändå lillördag.

Would you like a glass of wine? It is little Saturday.
