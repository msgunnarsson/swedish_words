Paltkoma

[ palt kaama ]

Food coma

Paltkoma is a dialectal expression from the north of Sweden and occurs when you've eaten a lot of (Pite)palt which is a traditional dish in the colder parts of Sweden. The expression is used all over the country for all over filled Swedes. You can also say “matkoma” (food coma), which has the same meaning.

Det här är den värsta paltkoman någonsin, jag har aldrig känt mig så här trött efter jag ätit lunch!

This is the worst paltkoma ever, I've never felt this tired after eating lunch!
