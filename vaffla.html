Våffla

[ vaafla ]

Waffle

The Swedish Waffle is better than most. It’s not as big and fat as Belgian, which (of course) packs the flavor better. Swedes enjoy their waffles in different ways: hot, straight out of the waffle iron, with cream and berry jam or, as some prefer, cold with butter the day after. Whether you like them hot or cold doesn’t matter, all you need to know is that no other country can match a Swedish Waffle. It even has it's own day, Våffeldagen (the waffle day).

Use this word in a sentence

En riktigt god våffla är varken torr eller degig, den är frasig.

A really tasty waffle is neither dry nor doughy, it’s crisp.
