Kräfta

[ kraefta ]

Crayfish / Crawlfish

What’s the ugliest thing you’ve ever eaten? If you want that record to be broken you should visit a Swedish crayfish party, not only will you get to slurp away on this weird seafood. You’ll also get the chance of seeing Swedish people going Cray… Because of all the booze they’ll be poring down their throats.

Use this word in a sentence

Svenskar blir som galna på kräfskivor.

The Swedes will go cray at crayfish parties.
