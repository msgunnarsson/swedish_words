Fredagsmys

Friday: the best of the working days. Hopefully ended by a few beers on the pub. Not so in Sweden, if you’re in a mature relationship (kids or no kids) Fridays are for mys (snuggling). But you have to be quick, or plan ahead, unless you want to find your local grocery store out of taco supplies. The whole family then consumes the taco goodness on the couch, while watching popular Friday entertainment, like That’s How it’s Supposed to Sound, The Prisoners in the Stronghold or Aiming for the Stars.
