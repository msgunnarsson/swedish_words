National anthem

The National Anthem, the pride of every inhabitant of a nation – except for Sweden. The national anthem is seen as nothing more than a patriotic semi racist propaganda song. If you sing it at any time other than during a football game, you should be ready for all the stink eyes people will give you.

”Du gamla, du fria” är Sveriges nationalsång.

“Du gamla, du fria” is Swedens national anthem.
