Cykel

[ sykkel ]

Bike

Where the English language chose to focus on the front, the Swedish language went with the back. From the word bicykel we wanted the part that actually meant something. Bi just means two, whereas cykel is something that goes round and round. But it’s when they’re put together that magic happens, sad to see that they’ve been separated in so many places.

Use this word in a sentence

Cyklandets popularitet går i cykler.

The popularity of cycling goes in cycles.
