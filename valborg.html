Walpurgis Night

Not only is the last day of April the Kings birthday. It’s also known as the most common night for teenage alcohol debuts. While celebrating the coming summer the teenagers celebrates as Swedes do, with alcohol. So if you ever want to see a lot of kids experience the sensation of alcohol for the first time, the 30th of April in Sweden is the time and place to be.
