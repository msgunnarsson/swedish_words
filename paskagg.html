Påskägg

[ paask egg ]

Easter egg

Påskäggs in Sweden are seldom large chocolate egg, or even crème eggs for that matter. A påskägg is instead hollow paper eggs filled with pick and mix candy. And as Easter is the mother of all candy holidays in Sweden, you better hope you get a large one. How else are you going to battle hunger during those long family gatherings?

Use this word in a sentence

Jag hittade första påskägget!

I found the first Easter egg!
