Ost

[ ósst ]

Cheese

Cheese is a joy and love of many people. But compared to the English the Swedes have a much more complicated palette of cheeses to choose from. It makes every trip to the supermarket a excruciating test of ones decision making skills. And if you want cheddar cheese – be prepared to buy it in the hamburger style packaging. No Swede with self respect would ever have it for breakfast.

Use this word in a sentence

Det godaste med osten är hålen.

The holes are the tasties part of the cheese.
