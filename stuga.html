Stuga

[ stuga ]

Cabin

Always in red with white corners and decorations, but the differences from the British cabins doesn’t stop there. The Swedish cabins are always wooden, never brick built. You’ll them find squeezed into clearings, battling coastal winds or overseeing fields and crops.

Use this word in a sentence

Svenska stugor är alltid röda.

Swedish cabins are always red.
