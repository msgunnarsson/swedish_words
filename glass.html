Glass

[ gláss ]

Ice cream

Although it might sound painful, we love eating glass in Sweden. It might be because it’s not what we use for windows, instead it’s the Swedish word for the cold, sweet treat known as ice cream.

Use this word in a sentence

Jag tittade ut genom fönstret och såg folk äta glass.

I watched through the window and saw people eating ice cream.
