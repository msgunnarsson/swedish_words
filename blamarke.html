Blåmärke

[ blaa-maerke ]

Bruise

A direct translation of the word blåmärke would be: blue mark. While there are many ways to get one, mountain biking in Wales is supposed to be one of the best ways.

Use this word in a sentence

Blåmärken kan vara blå, bruna och gula.

Bruises can be blue, brown and yellow.
