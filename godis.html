Godis

[ go-dees ]

Candy

Godis is a Swedish specialty. We might not have some of the fancy brand American candy, but we have the pick and mix. Invented in Finland and imported by Finnish students during the 1980s Sweden is now the biggest consumer of pick and mix candy in the world. And together with the Finns, we love salt licorice (salmiak or saltlakrits). Try it if you ever get the chance.

Use this word in a sentence

Är allt godis saltlaktits?

Is all the candy salt licorice?
