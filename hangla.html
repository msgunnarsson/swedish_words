Hångla

[ haangla ]

Make out

You could translate hångla into making out. But that wouldn’t tell the whole story. When you hånglar you’ve crossed the sensual/sexual line. It’s no longer pretty to watch, but all the more fun to participate in.

Use this word in a sentence

Ska vi hångla?

Want to make out?
