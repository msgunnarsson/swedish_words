Kanelbulle

[ kanélboulle ]

Cinnamon bun

The Kanelbulle is the staple of the Swedish fika and something every Swedishman and woman loves with their whole heart. If there’ll ever be a test for Swedish citizenship, the kanelbulle will surely be part of it.

Use this word in a sentence

En kanelbulle tack.

One cinnamon bun please.
