Kyckling

[ shykk ling ]

Chicken

As we all know a chicken is a bird; a young delicious one made for eating. Swedes love their chicken, just as any other people. But what they don’t care about is being called one. Swedes are patient and would only start a fight if their life depended on it. Long gone are the days when calling a Swede a chicken would mean a torching of your village, roasting of your children (like a chicken) and one way trip to Sweden for your wife.

Use this word in a sentence

Den här kycklingen är så rå att en skicklig veterinär kan återuppliva den.

This chicken is so raw a skilled vet could bring it back to life.

Pronunciation guide

For the weird Swedish letters.

ÅSay Aldershot and All, now just pronounce the A in those words. There it is! Å.

ÄEleven Element Elephant.

ÖSkirt Flirt Nerd Turd Bird Blur Scourge. Here, take the sound you’ve got before you say the R. Strip out the obvious letters in the beginning of the word and there it is. Ö.
