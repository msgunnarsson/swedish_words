Fika

[ fee-ka ]

Fika

Fika is both a Swedish verb and noun that roughly means “to drink coffee”, usually accompanied by something sweet. Have a break, have a fika.

Use this word in a sentence

Ska vi ta en fika?

Do you want to take fika?
