Vår

[ vaar ]

Spring

Swedes will start their hunt for the first signs of vår as soon as the new year is upon them. It’s often a fruitless hunt way into March. But as soon as the first buds are on the trees and the first wood anemone spotted, and instagrammed, the sun starved Swedes will come running out of their triple glassed houses.

Use this word in a sentence

Våren är vår, ungdomens och kärlekens årstid.

The spring is ours, the season of youth and love.
