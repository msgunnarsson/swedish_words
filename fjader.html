Feathers in every colour are key Easter decorations in Sweden. It fit’s well with the theme of the eggs, and reminds us all what the eggs could’ve been. But let’s forget that and munch away. Because the only thing that could actually ruin our Easter spirit is a drunken uncle flailing helplessly, as he trips on an imaginary wire trap set by all the brännvin he’s been drinking.
