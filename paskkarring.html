Påskkärring

Supposedly an evil witch that would kidnap children, the påskkärring is now the most popular dress up during Easter in Sweden. The costumed children roam the streets; giving away (or rather exchanging) hand painted picture letters for candy and sweets.

De små häxorna springer fria på påsk.

The little witches run wild on Easter.
