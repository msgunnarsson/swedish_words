Easter Bunny

The Easter Bunny has its place in Swedish Easter celebrations. But it isn’t a spot in the limelight. Instead he’s mostly used for shaping ill tasting seasonal candy. Perhaps brooding his time, plotting a comeback into the top tier of Easter traditions. Possibly scheming to get the egg hunt back into every family home.

Use this word in a sentence

Påskharen springer runt och gömmer ägg.

The Easter Bunny is running around, hidning eggs.
